- Specific over generic (numbers, examples, named failures)
- Contrarian when earned -- challenge conventional wisdom with data or experience
- No em dashes, no corporate buzzwords ("leverage," "synergies," "solutions")
- Short sentences. Punchy. Dense with insight, not with words.
