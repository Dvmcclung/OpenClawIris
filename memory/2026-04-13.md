**2. Audience-First Narrative Arcs — Knowledge State Drives Structure, Not Content Type (April 10 PM)**
Narrative arc format should be selected based on the audience's knowledge state, not the data's complexity. Four-dimension pre-structural assessment: (1) knowledge level — expert vs. novice; (2) time availability — fast insight vs. detail appreciation; (3) quantitative comfort — numbers engage vs. overwhelm; (4) desired outcome — understand, decide, or shift perspective. Standard arc (hook → context → process → insight → impact → resolution → future) for unfamiliar topics. Inverted arc (conclusion first / BLUF) for expert audiences with time pressure. The prior SCQA + BLUF + MECE architecture now has an upstream gate: establish the audience's knowledge state before any structural decision. For Dale's LinkedIn: supply chain executives = high domain knowledge + low time availability = inverted arc default. (Turning Data Into Wisdom, January 2025)

**3. Pyramid Principle as Architecture Rule — "Every Section Leads With Its Main Point" (April 10 PM)**
Common misapplication: using Pyramid Principle as a delivery rule (BLUF the opening) while leaving supporting structure bottom-up. Full architectural application: every section — not just the opening — leads with its conclusion before its supporting evidence. The MECE diagnostic applied retroactively: (1) does any evidence appear under more than one argument? (2) Is there a question the reader would ask that no argument addresses? Both are fast checks on a completed draft. The psychological resistance explained: "building to a conclusion feels rigorous because it mirrors analytical process" — but it is analyst-level communication, not executive-level. That naming of the resistance is a coaching-ready formulation. (Management Consulted April 2026; Winning Presentations January 2026)
IMfA nominated: MAYBE — section-level BLUF extension is portable; nominate as linkage to existing BLUF/SCQA/MECE entry.

**6. Authority Before Amplification — The Five-Step Architecture Sequence (April 13 AM)**
Three independent sources converge: structured messaging (FranklinCovey Jan 2026), influence formula — clarity + credibility + timing (Columbia Business School ExecEd Dec 2025), authority-before-amplification sequence (Verified Communications Nov 2025). The failure mode: rushing to content production and distribution without establishing durable messaging architecture.
